ABOUT

  • WHY OUR LEMON?

    Our preservative-free lemonades are made with fresh lemons, bottled within 24 hours of harvest. Pre-order for maximum freshness. Our distinct commitment to freshness sets us apart. Our goal: natural lemon flavor enhanced by our special recipe.

  • WHY KANTUS?

    KANTUS is the graceful white stallion on our horse farm that happens to be dad’s favourite horse. Our lemon trees live off the nutrients from the compost produced by him and his friends. Without him, our lemons would not be so tasty!

  • WHAT MAKES OUR LEMONS SPECIAL?

    Our Eureka lemons are known for their fresh and tangy taste. It would be hard to find a more perfect home for them than here, facing a large lake and close to the sea. A sea breeze nourishes the lemons while the lake provides a ready supply of clean water.
